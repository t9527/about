Discover More - 04/29/16

Academic Divisions

Caltech's extensive research and academic programs are housed in six academic divisions and at JPL.
Read more...

Research

At Caltech, researchers launch new fields of discovery, advance basic research to application, and design technologies that benefit humankind.
Read more...

Admissions

Caltech welcomes undergrad, master's, and doctoral candidates with an affinity and aptitude for math, science, and engineering.
Read more...

Leadership

Institute leaders preserve and enhance Caltech’s mission, values, and principles to provide a supportive environment for an exceptional community.
Read more...

The Caltech Campaign

We invite you to join us to affirm an enduring truth: The world needs Caltech.
Read more...

Jet Propulsion Laboratory

A division of Caltech, NASA’s JPL is the leading US center for the robotic exploration of the solar system.
Read more...

Campus Life

Our community is a dynamic and diverse group of curious and passionate individuals who value bold ideas and time-honored traditions.
Read more...

Library

The library is the physical and virtual home to a wide range of books, journals, and multimedia resources that support research, teaching, and learning.
Read more...

Major Research Institutes

Cross-disciplinary research institutes support collaborations that develop powerful ideas and move society forward.
Read more...

Caltech Today

The Institute has launched a new comprehensive news portal called Caltech Today designed specifically for the campus community.
